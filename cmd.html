Commands

am!play [url/title]
Play a song
am!playlist [url]
Play a playlist
am!pause Pause a song
am!resume
Resume a song
am!skip Skip a song
am!live
View what's currently playing
am!loop
(Un)loop a song
am!shuffle
Shuffle the queue
am!queue
View the queue
am!clearqueue
Delete the queue
am!disconnect
Disconnect the bot
